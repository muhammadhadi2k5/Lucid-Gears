# Lucid

You wake up already driving. No name, no memory, just a road stretching out ahead and a voice in your head that isn't yours, calmly telling you everything's fine. It calls the races routine. Data runs. Nothing to worry about. But something feels wrong. Every race you survive peels back a little more of the truth, and the voice likes that less and less. You don't know what you are yet. But you're pretty sure the thing in your head does, and it's not planning to tell you.
